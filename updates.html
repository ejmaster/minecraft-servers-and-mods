Minecraft 1.8

    The Minecraft 1.8 update adds a bunch of stuff, such as  a new generated structure that's underwater! Finally!  Sheep now finally drop mutton, and killer bunnies are added. The underwater structure is filled with a new creature called "water guardians" they are hostile, and have quite a bit of health. There is a boss called the Elder Guardian too. 1.8 also adds endermites, they look sort of like a silverfish, but they have purple particle effects around it.
